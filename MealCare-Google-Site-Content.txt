SECTION: SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

Heading:
System requirements

Body:
• Device: A computer, tablet, or smartphone with a modern web browser.
• Browser: Latest version of Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, or Safari.
• Internet: A stable internet connection is required. MealCare runs in the browser and uses online services for sign-in and data.
• Account: You need a MealCare user account (email and password) created at registration or provided by your project team.

Note for markers / technical readers:
MealCare is a single-page web application (React, Vite) with authentication and data services hosted in the cloud (e.g. Supabase). End users do not install software on their device beyond using a web browser.

SECTION: HOW TO USE MEALCARE
================================================================================

Heading:
How to use MealCare

Intro:
After you sign in, use the navigation bar at the top to move between sections. Names may vary slightly, but they match the app.

Feature list (match your app):
• Home — Overview and entry point after signing in.
• Meal Plan — Build or adjust your meal plan.
• View meal plan — See your planned meals; open a meal for more detail when available.
• Shopping List — Items to buy based on your plan (or as you add them).
• Profile — Your details and preferences.
• Caregiving — For users acting as a caregiver: manage links and view the care recipient’s plan when your account has permission. If you do not see Caregiving, your account may not be set up as a caregiver.
• Account / Log in — Sign in when logged out; account-related options when logged in.

Caregiver note (navbar “Viewing”):
If you are a caregiver and linked to someone, you may see a “Viewing” control to switch between yourself and a care recipient. Choose the person whose meal plan and lists you want to see.

Sign out:
Use Sign out when you finish on a shared device.

SECTION: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
================================================================================

Heading:
Frequently asked questions

Q: Do I need to install an app from an app store?
A: No. MealCare runs in your web browser. You only need the website link and an account.

Q: Which browsers work?
A: Use an up-to-date version of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari.

Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
A: On the login page, use the password reset or “Forgot password” option if available, and follow the email instructions.

Q: Can I use MealCare on my phone?
A: Yes, if your phone has a supported browser and internet access. The layout is designed for common screen sizes; a tablet or computer can be easier for longer planning sessions.

Q: Why don’t I see Caregiving?
A: Caregiving appears only for accounts set up as caregivers with linked care recipients.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: MealCare uses secure sign-in and cloud services. For project-specific or institutional privacy information, contact your team or supervisor.

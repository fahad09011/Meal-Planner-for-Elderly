Welcome to MealCare

Meal planning for older adults and caregivers: plan meals, view your plan, use your shopping list, and update your profile. Caregivers can use Caregiving when linked to a care recipient.

Open MealCare

Live app: https://meal-planner-for-elderly.vercel.app/

System requirements

  • Device: Computer, tablet, or smartphone with a modern web browser.
  • Browser: Recent Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari.
  • Internet: Stable connection required (MealCare runs online).
  • Account: Email and password (register or use details from your project team).

No app install from an app store — MealCare opens in the browser.

How to access MealCare

  1. Open your browser.
  2. Go to https://meal-planner-for-elderly.vercel.app/
  3. Choose Log in and sign in, or register if your team has enabled it.

Meal Plan

Pick a day, choose breakfast, lunch, and dinner, then save. Use Quick actions to suggest the whole week when you are ready.

View meal plan

See what is planned for the week, mark meals as eaten, and open recipes.

Shopping List

Ingredients come from your saved plan. Filter by aisle, tick items as you shop, and watch your progress.

Profile

Your user ID (for caregivers), body and activity settings, and food or health preferences.

How to use MealCare

After you sign in, use the top navigation:

  • Home — Start screen.
  • Meal Plan — Build or change your plan.
  • View meal plan — See planned meals; open a meal for details.
  • Shopping List — Items to buy.
  • Profile — Your details.
  • Caregiving — For caregivers with linked recipients (hidden if not set up).
  • Account / Log in — Sign in or account options.

Caregivers may see Viewing to switch between yourself and a care recipient. Use Sign out on shared devices.

Frequently asked questions

  • Do I install an app? No — use the link in a browser.
  • Forgot password? Use the reset option on the login page and check email.
  • Phone? Yes, with a supported browser and internet.
  • No Caregiving menu? Your account may not be set up as a caregiver.
